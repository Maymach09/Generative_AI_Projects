## Test Scenarios for Insurance Claims Processing System

### Electronic Claims Submission and Intake

1. **Valid Claim Submission:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a valid claim with complete and accurate information.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The claim is successfully submitted and validated.

2. **Incomplete Claim Submission:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim with missing or incomplete information.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system flags the missing information and prompts the user to complete the claim.

3. **Invalid Claim Submission:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim with invalid data, such as an incorrect date of birth or service.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system rejects the claim and provides an error message.

### Claims Processing and Benefit Calculation

4. **Standard Claim Processing:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim that meets all standard processing criteria.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The claim is processed automatically and benefits are calculated based on the defined rules.

5. **Complex Claim Processing:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim with complex eligibility criteria or special circumstances.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system applies the appropriate rules and calculates benefits accurately.

6. **Benefit Calculation Verification:**
    - **Scenario:** Verify the accuracy of benefit calculations for various claim scenarios.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The calculated benefits are consistent with the defined rules and regulations.

### Coordination of Benefits

7. **Multiple Insurance Providers:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim with coverage from multiple insurance providers.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system coordinates benefits effectively and ensures that the correct amount is paid by each provider.

8. **Coordination of Benefits Rules:**
    - **Scenario:** Test the system's ability to apply different coordination of benefits rules based on the specific circumstances.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system correctly applies the appropriate rules to determine the order of payment and the amount paid by each provider.

### Fraud Detection

9. **Automated Fraud Detection:**
    - **Scenario:** Submit a claim with potential fraudulent indicators, such as duplicate claims or suspicious billing patterns.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system flags the claim for further investigation.

10. **Fraud Detection Rules:**
    - **Scenario:** Test the system's ability to identify fraudulent claims based on various rules and algorithms.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system accurately identifies fraudulent claims and provides appropriate alerts.

### Manual Claim Adjustments

11. **Manual Claim Adjustment:**
    - **Scenario:** Manually adjust a claim based on specific circumstances or errors.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system allows for manual adjustments and updates the claim status accordingly.

12. **Claim Adjustment Audit Trail:**
    - **Scenario:** Verify that all claim adjustments are properly documented and auditable.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system maintains a complete audit trail of all claim adjustments.

### External System Integration

13. **EHR Integration:**
    - **Scenario:** Integrate with an external EHR system to retrieve patient information and claim details.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system seamlessly retrieves data from the EHR and populates the claim information.

14. **Insurance Provider Integration:**
    - **Scenario:** Integrate with external insurance provider systems to verify coverage and submit claims electronically.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system exchanges data with insurance providers efficiently and securely.

### Reporting and Analytics

15. **Claims Processing Reports:**
    - **Scenario:** Generate reports on claims processing metrics, such as claim volume, processing time, and rejection rates.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system provides comprehensive reports with relevant data and insights.

16. **Performance Analytics:**
    - **Scenario:** Analyze claims processing data to identify trends, bottlenecks, and areas for improvement.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system provides actionable insights to optimize claims processing efficiency and performance.

### Data Security and Audit Trails

17. **Data Encryption:**
    - **Scenario:** Verify that sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system ensures data security and compliance with industry standards.

18. **Audit Trail Functionality:**
    - **Scenario:** Test the system's ability to track all user actions and system events.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system maintains a complete and auditable trail of all activities.

### User Training and Support

19. **User Training Materials:**
    - **Scenario:** Evaluate the availability and effectiveness of user training materials.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system provides comprehensive and user-friendly training materials.

20. **Technical Support:**
    - **Scenario:** Test the responsiveness and effectiveness of technical support for users.
    - **Expected Outcome:** The system provides timely and helpful support to users.
