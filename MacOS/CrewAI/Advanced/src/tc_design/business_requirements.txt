A Claims Adjudication System should enable electronic claims submission and intake, validating completeness and accuracy. 
It must process claims using a robust rules engine for benefit calculation, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements. 
The system should support coordination of benefits, automate fraud detection, and facilitate manual claim adjustments. 
Integration with external systems (e.g., EHR, insurers) and secure data exchange is critical. 
It should generate detailed reports and analytics on claims processing and performance. 
Ensuring data security, maintaining audit trails, and providing user training and support are essential for effective 
operation and compliance with industry standards.
